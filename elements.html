Traffic problems between Sydney and Newcastle

Problem definition

According to the analysis in Assignment 2, the number of people in coastal areas who choose to drive to work is higher and the growth rate is higher.
Sydney, as Australia's most densely populated area, traffic congestion has always been a problem that cannot be ignored.Newcastle is the second largest city in New South Wales, about 180 kilometers from the centre of Sydney.It takes about 2.5 hours.
If a bridge is built along the coastline, the distance will be shortened to 115 kilometers, and closed highways can increase the speed limit of the road. On this basis, the time required to reach Newcastle from Sydney will be reduced from two hours to one hour.

Three elements

Highways,Urban viaducts, shopping malls


By combining these three elements, you can create a new way to solve the city congestion problem.
For large international cities like Sydney, traffic jams are an urgent problem to solve. A section of highway or railway leading to other cities can divert people, and it also provides people with more convenient modes of travel while reducing traffic pressure. Shopping malls along the way can facilitate people's shopping needs when traveling, and can be accompanied by hotels and other functions, a large sea view hotel located above the mall can provide people with a good holiday or travel environment.

Proposal & Proposal

The aim of this project is to provide people with a new way of life. We can choose to live in a countryside away from the city. At the same time, it is also in the urban one-hour life circle. In an hour, people can travel from the country road to the urban CBD. The enclosed bridge will connect Sydney with Newcastle and Gosford, and it will also have two large malls with gas stations. Gosford can undoubtedly take advantage of the transportation advantages of these two cities to drive its own economic development. In these two oval sea buildings, the ground floor can be used as a gas station space, the middle part can be used as a shopping mall space, and the upper space can be used as a hotel space. The design of the double-deck lane reduces its traffic pressure, and its split-type secondary exit can also reduce the pressure of urban traffic as the role of urban viaducts.The lower lane can be laid with train tracks to improve the efficiency of transportation.
